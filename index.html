Legal Services
We Handle

📜

Conveyancing

The legal process of transferring property ownership from one party to another, ensuring all legal requirements and documentation are properly completed.

  • Agreement of Sale
  • Gift Deed
  • Lease Deed
  • Partnership Deed
  • Will Drafting
  • Rent Agreement
  • Licensee Assignment
⚖️

Civil Litigation

Civil litigation is a legal process where individuals or entities resolve non-criminal disputes in court, often involving issues like contracts, property, or personal injuries.

🏛️

Real Estate

Real estate law governs the legal aspects of property ownership, transactions, disputes, and development, ensuring compliance with regulations and protecting property rights.

🖊️

Registration

Registration services involve the legal process of formally recording property, business, or legal documents with government authorities to establish official ownership or rights.

🔒

Criminal Cases

Criminal cases involve legal proceedings where an individual is charged with committing a crime, and the state prosecutes to enforce criminal laws and seek justice.

🛡️

Consumer Cases

Consumer protection cases address legal disputes where consumers seek remedies for unfair practices, fraud, or defective products to ensure their rights are upheld.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Law

Family law cases deal with legal matters involving domestic relationships, such as divorce, child custody, alimony, adoption, and guardianship.

  • Succession & Probate
  • Maintenance
  • Partition Suit
  • Injunction
  • Possession Suit
  • Recovery Suits
🤝

Arbitration

Arbitration is an alternative dispute resolution process where a neutral third party delivers a binding decision to resolve conflicts outside of the court system.

🏭

Employee Compensation Claims

Workers who suffer injury, disability, or death in the course of employment are entitled to compensation under the Employees' Compensation Act, 1923. Representation before the Commissioner for Employees' Compensation.

🚗

Accident Claim Cases

Victims of road accidents and their families are entitled to compensation under the Motor Vehicles Act. Representation before the Motor Accidents Claims Tribunal (MACT).
